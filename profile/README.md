# R3ISE Research Team

**R3ISE (Research in Resilient, Intelligent Software Engineering)** is a university-based research group focused on advancing the foundations and practice of modern software systems through rigorous research, open science, and student-driven innovation.

Our research lies at the intersection of **software engineering**, **distributed systems**, and **artificial intelligence**, with particular emphasis on:

- Observability, explainability, and monitoring of microservices
- AI-assisted software engineering (LLMs, RAG, knowledge graphs)
- Adaptive and data-driven DevOps and MLOps
- Software architecture comprehension and evolution
- Reproducible and sustainable research software

We aim to bridge **theory and practice**, producing artifacts that are both scientifically rigorous and practically impactful.
